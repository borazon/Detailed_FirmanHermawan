Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Boosting luxury smartphone sales

Based on the dashboard data, this segment recorded the lowest sales figures. Therefore, a well-formulated strategy is needed to drive its sales growth.

Solution:

Given the substantial budget spent by customers, we can offer limited-quantity bonuses of equivalent value. Sales data from other categories (Accessories) shows that chargers, wireless headsets, and covers have relatively high sales contributions. These three items can serve as meaningful bonuses that match the customer’s investment, especially during pre-order periods or special promotional events.
