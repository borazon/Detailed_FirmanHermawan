Project Overview

This project aims to analyze job vacancy data throughout 2022 sourced from the Jobstreet platform. The main focus is to identify the most in-demand job roles, the most active industry sectors, and the companies with the highest number of job postings.

Visualization Types

Donut-Chart

This donut chart displays the most in-demand job categories based on company postings.

Bar-Chart

This bar chart serves as supporting data to identify the companies with the highest number of job postings.
